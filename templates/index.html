Insect Symbionts Genome Database

This database contains genome sequences obtained mostly from various public sources (e.g, NCBI and EMBL) as well as some additional data produced specifically for our own projects. Annotation has been enhances with transcriptomic, proteomics and metabolomic data in order to produce models of the metabolism of these organisms and their hosts.


This site was developed with funding from a BBSRC new investigator award to Dr. Alistair Darby, University of Liverpool. The software was developed by Dr. Peter Ashton, University of York.
